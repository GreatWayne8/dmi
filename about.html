About Us

Join Us in Making a Difference to the World

We believe in God the Father, the Creator of heaven and earth. We believe God made us for His purpose, to live in relationship with Him. He richly provides for our every need and sustains all life by His word.

We believe in Jesus Christ, the Son of God, who was born of the Virgin Mary, suffered, died, was buried, and rose again on the third day. He sits at the right hand of God and reigns with authority over all creation.

We believe that salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone — not by works but by His sacrifice, death, and resurrection. By His blood, we are forgiven, redeemed, and made righteous.

We believe in the Holy Spirit, who calls, enlightens, sanctifies, and preserves us in the true faith. The Holy Spirit empowers us for ministry and unites us as one body in Christ.

OUR MISSION

To raise believers who boldly defend the truth, live intentionally, and impact nations through the gospel of Jesus Christ.

  • We believe in the authority of God’s Word.
  • We believe Jesus Christ is Savior and Lord.
  • We believe in the power of the Holy Spirit.
  • We believe in equipping disciples to impact the world.
  • We believe in building strong families and godly communities.
