Apostle Ben Achebe
Lead Pastor & Visionary

Apostle Ben Achebe is the founder and visionary leader of Defenders Ministry International. With a Master of Divinity from -----, he has served in full-time ministry for over a decade. His calling is to raise a generation of bold, Spirit-empowered disciples and to advance the Kingdom of God across nations. Known for his dynamic preaching, apostolic insight, and passion for revival, he continues to equip leaders and ignite purpose in the Body of Christ.

Pastor Winnie Achebe
Pastor

Pastor Winnie Achebe serves faithfully alongside her husband, Apostle Achebe, as the co-laborer in ministry. She leads Bible studies, discipleship classes, and women’s empowerment programs. With a heart for mentoring and a passion for spiritual growth, she nurtures both individuals and families in the Word and love of God.

Karisa Eric
MOP (Men of Purpose)

Karisa Eric leads the Men of Purpose (MOP) ministry, empowering men to walk boldly in their faith, lead with integrity, and impact their families and communities. He mentors men in spiritual growth, accountability, and kingdom leadership.

Loice Mukami
Follow Ups

Loice ensures that every new believer and visitor feels connected and cared for. She oversees follow-up ministries, coordinates calls and visits, and supports people in their spiritual journey beyond the altar call.

Caren Chemutai
Projects & Development

Caren spearheads ministry infrastructure, outreach programs, and expansion projects. Her focus is to turn vision into action—planning, organizing, and executing developments that advance the mission of DMI on a structural and societal level.

Sylvia Rono
Chaplaincy

Sylvia provides pastoral care, counseling, and spiritual support to individuals in hospitals, schools, and other institutions. As a chaplain, she brings the presence of Christ into places of need, offering hope, prayer, and healing.
